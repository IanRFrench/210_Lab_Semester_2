Chocolate Chip Cookies

Best Homemade Recipe

Home Recipes Contact

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Servings: 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  5. Bake 8–10 minutes.
